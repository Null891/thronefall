Nordfels Visual System

A low-poly island kingdom in an open sea, seen from a high angle. No outlines — depth comes from three-tone face shading (top, front, side) on every block, plus soft radial shadow pools. The whole palette re-keys between day and night, sea included; gold is reserved for money, victory, and anything the player may press.

Every material ships as three tones with a deliberate hue shift — lit tops lean warm, shadowed faces cool toward teal and violet instead of merely darkening. Day/night is a single token swap, never a repaint. Threat colors are locked and unshaded (red #E64A53, toxic green #85D671) so enemies stay parseable in any light.

Motion rules

  • Ambient — clouds drift, flags wave, mill blades spin, slimes squish: 0.5–20s loops, all disabled under prefers-reduced-motion.
  • Day → night — one 1.1s palette cross-fade on every face token; stars, window-light and torch glow fade in on the same clock.
  • The King — gallop bob at 0.28s while moving, dust puffs behind the hooves; the camera never cuts.
  • Feedback — buildings pop in with a 0.35s overshoot; damage numbers rise and fade; deaths are one expanding ring, no gore.
  • Cooldowns — a conic sweep drains clockwise; the button rim flashes gold when ready.
